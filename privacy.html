Privacy Policy for Screenshot2Pin

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

Screenshot2Pin is a localized, non-commercial productivity utility built exclusively to facilitate personal design research and visual asset management.

1. Data Collection and Transmission

This application operates entirely end-to-end on the user's local hardware workspace environment. The application does not collect, track, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, screen captures, images, or account credentials to any external servers or third-party entities outside of the official, secure Pinterest API endpoints.

2. Third-Party API Integration

Our integration interacts solely with official Pinterest Developer Platform endpoints to facilitate board selection and authorized Pin creation. All authentication data is processed locally via standard encrypted OAuth tokens.

3. Compliance and Inquiries

This utility complies with data minimization principles. For any legal or developer compliance inquiries, please contact the developer via the open-source product hosting repository.
